FAQ's

FAQ Accordion
How long does siding typically last? ▶
The lifespan of siding depends on the material and maintenance. Vinyl siding can last 20 to 40 years, while fiber cement siding often lasts 50 years or more.
Can new siding improve energy efficiency? ▶
Yes! Modern siding materials help insulate your home, which can reduce heating and cooling costs.
What services does Siding & Window Solutions offer? ▶
Siding & Window Solutions specializes in providing high-quality siding and window installations, replacements, and repairs.
